About Konix Markit

Konix Markit brings quality products at fair prices to shoppers across Ghana. We curate reliable items, support local delivery partners, and aim to make shopping simple and trustworthy.

Our Story

Founded to make everyday shopping easier, Konix Markit started as a small local store and grew into a community-focused marketplace. We combine carefully selected products with friendly local support to help you find what you need.

Our Mission

Deliver quality products, transparent pricing, and fast local delivery across Ghana. We prioritise durability, value, and customer-first policies.

Quality
We test and vet products.
Value
Fair prices and clear fees.
Support
Local delivery and friendly help.

Who we serve

Households, small businesses, and everyday shoppers across urban and regional Ghana. We focus on accessible service and dependable fulfilment.
